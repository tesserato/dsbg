The quest to replace Authy desktop

Created: 2024-05-28

Recently, or not that much, Authy decided to discontinue its desktop auth. I've been using it for quite a long time, and was actually quite happy with it. Nevertheless, had to start searching for a new app. Long story short, I think I'll be settling for the Proton Pass app for now. The real contenders apparently boil down to three:

  • Proton Pass
  • Zoho
  • Ente Auth
