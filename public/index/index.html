<h1 class="title"> &#10026; Research Paper Structure &#10026; Carlos Tarjano &#10026; tesserato@hotmail.com</h1>

<ol>
  <li><strong>Claim:</strong> What do you want me to believe? What’s your point?</li>
  <li><strong>Reasons:</strong> Why do you say that? Why should I agree?</li>
  <li><strong>Evidence:</strong> How do you know? Can you back it up?</li>
  <li><strong>Warrant:</strong> How does that follow? What’s your logic? Can you explain your reasoning?</li>
  <li><strong>Acknowledgment and Response:</strong> But what about . . . ?</li>
</ol>

<details id="literature-review">
  <summary>Literature review | Brief Context of Prior Research</summary>
  <ul>
    <li>put your research in the context of other research</li>
    <li>Nature and scope of the problem investigated</li>
    <li>give them a framework for understanding it</li>
    <li>What is the problem to be solved?</li>
    <li>Are there any existing solutions?</li>
    <li>Which is the best?</li>
    <li>What is its main limitation?</li>
    <li>What do you hope to achieve?</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details id="question">

  </ul>
</details>
